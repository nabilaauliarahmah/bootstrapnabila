Information

STRATEGIC

Geographically, Indonesia is located between two continents (Asia and Australia) and two oceans (Indonesian Ocean and Pacific Ocean), which is the point of intersection of the world. Consists of approximately 17,000 islands which are a unified whole.

CLIMATE

Overall, Indonesia's climate is tropical. This means that the climate in Indonesia is relatively warm, tends to be hot and humid throughout the year. With this tropical climate, Indonesia only has two seasons, namely the dry season and the rainy season.

EXOTIC

Indonesia is an archipelagic country consisting of various ethnic groups, cultures and customs. Indonesia has 8 UNESCO-listed cultural heritage sites. Indonesia also has abundant natural resources and is a center of world trade.

Angklung

Angklung is a typical Indonesian musical instrument originating from the West Java area. This traditional musical instrument is made of bamboo tubes. While the sound or tone of this tool is produced from the impact of the bamboo tube by shaking it.

Kecak Dance

Kecak dance is a dance art originating from Bali. The art of this Kecak dance is performed by dozens of male dancers who wear plaid cloth and sit in a circle in a circular pattern and with a certain rhythm call “cak, cak, cak” and raise both hands.
